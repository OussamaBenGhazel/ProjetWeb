Reviews

Colleen Hoover

Colleen Hoover is an American author of romance and young adult novels. Hoover's novels often deal with heavy themes such as abuse, addiction, and mental health, and she is known for her ability to create complex, flawed characters that resonate with readers.

Louisa May Alcott

In addition to Little Women, Alcott wrote several other novels and numerous short stories, poems, and essays. Her works often explore themes of family, love, and social justice

Tess Sharpe

Tess Sharpe is known for writing stories with strong, complex female protagonists who often face difficult challenges and navigate complicated relationships.

Kristin Hannah

Hannah's novels often focus on relationships, family dynamics, and the experiences of women, and she is known for her ability to create emotionally powerful stories that resonate with readers.
